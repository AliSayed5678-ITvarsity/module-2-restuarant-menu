A sundae is an ice cream frozen dessert of American origin. It typically consists of one or more scoops of ice cream topped with sauce or syrup and various delightful toppings. These toppings can include sprinkles, whipped cream, marshmallows, peanuts, maraschino cherries, and even fruits like bananas and pineapple (as seen in a classic banana split).
